# SLEEPLESS
## Executive Summary

---

### The Company

**Sleepless** is South Africa's premier event discovery and ticketing platform, connecting millions of entertainment seekers with the events they love. From daytime festivals and pool parties to evening concerts and late-night experiences, Sleepless is the one-stop destination for discovering, booking, and experiencing events across all nine provinces.

---

### The Opportunity

| Market Metric | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| Total Addressable Market | R40+ billion |
| Target Users (18-45) | 18 million |
| Annual Market Growth | 8-12% |
| Current Digital Penetration | <15% |

South Africa's entertainment market is massive yet fragmented, with no dominant mobile platform serving event discovery and ticketing.

---

### The Problem

**For Event-Goers:** Events are scattered across social media, discovery is fragmented, ticket fraud is rampant, and there's no personalized experience.

**For Organizers:** Limited reach beyond existing followers, manual ticketing processes, zero analytics, and high no-show rates.

---

### Our Solution

A comprehensive mobile platform featuring smart event discovery, secure QR-coded tickets, seamless payments, social features, and powerful organizer tools—all in one beautiful app.
