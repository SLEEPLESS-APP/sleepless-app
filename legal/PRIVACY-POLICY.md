## 2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about Users of our Services, including personal information that can be used to identify you.

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register for an account, purchase tickets, or use certain features of the Services, you may provide us with the following information:

**Account Information:** Your name, email address, username, password, phone number, date of birth, and profile picture.

**Payment Information:** Credit card number, billing address, and other payment details. Payment information is processed by third-party payment processors and is not stored on our servers.

**Event Organizer Information:** If you register as an Event Organizer, you may provide additional information such as company name, business registration number, tax identification number, bank account details, and event-related content (event descriptions, images, venue information).

**User-Generated Content:** Comments, reviews, ratings, messages, and other content you submit through the Services.

**Communications:** Information you provide when you contact us for customer support, feedback, or inquiries.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access or use the Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

**Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers (UDID, IMEI), mobile network information, and IP address.

**Usage Data:** Pages viewed, features accessed, time spent on the Services, search queries, event views, ticket purchases, and interaction with notifications.

**Location Information:** With your permission, we may collect precise geolocation data from your device to provide location-based features such as nearby events and venue directions. You can disable location services in your device settings at any time.

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, including:

**Social Media Platforms:** If you choose to log in using Google or Apple Sign-In, we may receive your name, email address, profile picture, and other information you authorize the platform to share.

**Event Organizers:** Event Organizers may provide us with information about ticket purchases, event attendance, and customer interactions.

**Analytics Providers:** We use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics) to understand how Users interact with the Services.
